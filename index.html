Fluffy Dog Rescue

Thank you for considering fostering a pup in need. Your temporary love and care can make a lasting impact on a dog's life. Check out our available foster dogs and fill out an application to get started.

Why Foster?

Save Lives

Fostering gives dogs a chance at finding their forever homes and opens up space for more dogs in the shelter.

Teach Socialization

Dogs who are fostered learn how to socialize with people and other animals, making them more adoptable

Experience

Fostering provides an oppertunity to learn about different breeds, personalities and behaviors, preparing you for future pet ownership.

Requirements

Time Commitment

Fostering can range from a few weeks to several months, depending on the dog's specific needs.

Environment

You don't necessarily need a big home or yard, but space and safety are key factors when fostering. Providing a healthy, safe and stable enviroment for the dog is crucial.

Support System

Having a support system in place, like other family members or close friends, is important when fostering a dog, It helps to have someone to help out if needed or just to share the experience with

How it Works

Step 1: Apply

Fill out an application and a foster coordinator will contact you and guide you through the process.

Step 2: Match

We'll help you find a dog whose personality and energy level is a good match for your home

Step 3: Foster

You'll provide a temporary home for the dog, including food, shelter, exercise, and love.

Step 4: Adoption

We'll help you find a permanent home for the dog that fits their needs and personality.

Benefits

  1. Flexible

    As a foster parent, you can choose when you are willing to take in a dog and how often you want to foster.

  2. Free Supplies

    We provide all necessary medical care and supplies for your foster dog.

  3. Joyful Experience

    Fostering a dog is extremely rewarding and can make a huge difference in their life

FAQs

How long does the fostering period last?

The fostering period can vary depending on the dog. Some dogs need to stay in foster care for several months while others can be adopted within weeks.

What happens if I can't keep fostering?

If you can no longer foster the dog, we'll work with you to find another suitable temporary home for the dog.

Do I need to have prior dog experience?

No prior experience is required. We provide training and resources to help you be a successful foster parent.
